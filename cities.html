How to use the service

Click on "View cities" to view the current list of cities.

To add a city to the list, enter a city ID, a city name and a population, choose "Add city" from the dropdown menu and click "Submit". If you don't enter an ID, one will be created for you.

To edit a city, choose "Edit city" from the dropdown menu, enter the ID of the city you wish to edit, enter the information you'd like to change and click "Submit".

To delete a city, choose "Delete city" from the dropdown menu, enter the ID of the city you wish to delete and click "Submit"

Click on "View Cities" at any time to see the updated list of cities
